About EFY

EFY (Expiryfy) is a smart shelf monitoring system that helps shopkeepers and supermarkets track the expiry dates of products in real time. The system sends notifications 3 days before expiry, allowing staff to take action early. Using shelf rack mapping, EFY identifies exactly where the product is located, saving time and reducing losses. With EFY, businesses can ensure consumer safety, reduce food wastage, and maintain trust.
